About

Let me introduce myself.

I am a PhD Candidate in the Pattern Recognition and Optimisation group in the Faculty of Engineering and IT at the University of Melbourne. My primary research interests lie in Deep Neural Networks and Bioinformatics.

I am also working as an academic tutor at the University of Melbourne. I was previously employed as a Junior Lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.
